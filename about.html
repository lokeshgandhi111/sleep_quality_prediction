About Our Project

Welcome to Sleep Quality Prediction, a tool designed to help you understand and improve your sleep patterns using data-driven insights. Our platform combines sleep science with machine learning to provide personalized recommendations for better sleep.

Our Mission

We aim to empower individuals to achieve better sleep by providing personalized predictions and recommendations based on their daily habits, lifestyle, and sleep environment. In today's fast-paced world, quality sleep is often compromised, leading to numerous health issues. We're here to change that.

Our team of sleep specialists and data scientists have developed algorithms that learn from your patterns to help you make informed decisions about your sleep health.

How It Works

Our prediction model analyzes various factors that influence your sleep quality to provide accurate predictions and actionable insights:

Sleep Duration

Analyzes your total sleep time and consistency of sleep schedule

Bedtime Routine

Examines pre-sleep activities that affect your ability to fall asleep

Physical Activity

Evaluates how exercise impacts your sleep quality

Stress Levels

Measures psychological factors affecting sleep

Caffeine & Alcohol

Tracks substance consumption and its timing relative to bedtime

Environment

Assesses noise, light, temperature and comfort factors

Using advanced machine learning algorithms, we predict sleep quality and suggest personalized steps to enhance restfulness based on your unique patterns.
